Our DNA Experiment!

In our DNA project,I worked with Sophie and Mehreen, we aimed to test our DNA and see how many times it repeats. Also, we wanted to see and predict who we would have matched with. To do this. In specific, we did DNA extraction, which is the process of purifying DNA from biological samples by breaking open cell membranes and separating the genetic material from proteins and other contaminants. Our process was simple: get a 50 ul tube, a buffer and a flat toothpick. Next, scrape the side of your cheek 3-4 times with the toothpick. Then, dip the end of the toothpick into the buffer and swirl it and secure the tube. After, Heat the tube using either Heat Block mode, a water bath, or other heat block. And finally, Remove the tube from the heat and immediately use DNA extract for PCR. This process tested our DNA. After a couple of days, we received our results and it did surprise us and my hypothesis was incorrect. I hypothesized that I would match with people that are from the middle east/ neighboring countries.
